Professional Notarization — In-Person or Remote

Whether you need a single document notarized or an entire loan package handled, our professional notary services provide fast, accurate, and legally compliant notarization in Fayetteville, NC and remotely across states where Remote Online Notarization (RON) is permitted.

Documents We Notarize

  • Legal Documents: Affidavits, acknowledgments, jurats, sworn statements
  • Real Estate Documents: Deeds, mortgages, title transfers, closing packages
  • Estate Planning: Powers of attorney, wills, trusts, living wills, advance directives
  • Business Documents: Contracts, articles of organization, operating agreements, corporate resolutions
  • Loan Packages: Full mortgage and refinance loan signing packages
  • Personal Documents: Parental consent letters, vehicle titles, immigration documents
  • Financial Documents: Bank affidavits, authorization letters, financial statements

Remote Online Notarization (RON)

No time to come in? Remote Online Notarization allows documents to be notarized entirely online via secure audio-video technology — valid in states that permit RON.

Requirements for RON:

  • Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport)
  • Stable internet connection and a device with camera and microphone
  • Documents must be suitable for electronic signatures and notarization
💡 Loan signing: We are trained and experienced loan signing agents. If you're closing on a home or refinancing, we can handle the entire signing package professionally and efficiently.

What to Bring to Your Appointment

  • All documents to be notarized — do NOT sign them before your appointment
  • Valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID
  • Any required witnesses (we can help arrange if needed)
  • Payment for notarization fees (varies by document type and state law)
