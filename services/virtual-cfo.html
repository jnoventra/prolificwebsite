Executive-level financial leadership for growing businesses — without the full-time cost of a CFO.

Think Like a Big Business. Stay Lean.

Growing businesses need strategic financial leadership — but most can't justify the $150,000–$250,000 cost of a full-time CFO. Our Virtual CFO service gives you executive-level financial expertise on a flexible, affordable engagement, so you can make smarter decisions, attract investors, and scale with confidence.

What a Virtual CFO Does for Your Business

  • Financial Forecasting & Budgeting: Build realistic forward-looking financial models so you know where your business is heading
  • Cash Flow Management: Monitor cash in and out, prevent shortfalls, and maintain healthy working capital
  • KPI Dashboards: Custom reports tracking the metrics that matter most for your specific business
  • Pricing Analysis: Ensure your products or services are priced for profitability, not just revenue
  • Profitability Analysis: Identify which products, services, or clients drive the most profit — and which don't
  • Investor & Lender Readiness: Prepare financial packages, projections, and presentations for funding conversations
  • Bank Relationship Management: Navigate loan applications, credit lines, and lender communications
  • Strategic Decision Support: Hire, expand, acquire, or exit — with financial data backing every major decision

Virtual CFO vs. Bookkeeper vs. Accountant

  • Bookkeeper: Records what happened — transactions, reconciliations, monthly reports
  • Accountant / Tax Preparer: Files your taxes and ensures compliance
  • Virtual CFO: Interprets all of it, looks forward, and guides strategic decisions to maximize profit and growth
💡 Is this right for you? Virtual CFO is ideal for growing startups, businesses approaching $500K–$5M in revenue, and owners preparing for a funding round, acquisition, or exit.

Deliverables You Can Expect

  • Rolling 12-month cash flow forecast
  • KPI scorecard updated monthly
  • Annual budget and variance tracking
  • Investor-ready financial model (upon request)
  • On-call advisory for major decisions
