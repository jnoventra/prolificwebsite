Know Your Numbers. Run a Better Business.

Most small business owners didn't start their company to spend hours on accounting. But without accurate, up-to-date financials, you're making decisions blind. Our bookkeeping and payroll services give you the financial clarity you need to grow — without the overhead of a full-time accountant.

Bookkeeping Services

  • Monthly Transaction Categorization: Every expense and income item properly categorized
  • Bank & Credit Card Reconciliation: Your records match your statements — every month
  • Accounts Receivable/Payable Tracking: Know who owes you and what you owe
  • QuickBooks Online Management: Setup, training, cleanup, and ongoing maintenance
  • Back-Book Cleanup: We fix messy or missing records from prior months or years
  • Monthly Financial Reports: P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, and custom dashboards
  • Tax-Ready Books: Your books are always ready for your tax preparer — no scramble in April

Payroll Services

  • Payroll Processing: Weekly, bi-weekly, or semi-monthly runs for W-2 employees
  • Payroll Tax Deposits: Federal and state payroll taxes deposited accurately and on time
  • Quarterly Payroll Reports: Form 941 (federal) and state equivalents
  • Annual W-2 & 1099 Filing: We prepare and file all year-end wage statements
  • New Hire Reporting: Compliance with state new hire reporting requirements
  • Direct Deposit Setup: Convenient, reliable pay delivery for your team
💡 Platforms we support: QuickBooks Online is our primary platform. We also work with other leading accounting software — ask us about your current setup.

Monthly Reports You'll Actually Use

  • Profit & Loss Statement: Revenue vs. expenses — your business scorecard
  • Balance Sheet: Assets, liabilities, and equity at a glance
  • Cash Flow Statement: Track actual cash in and out
  • Accounts Receivable Aging: Who owes you money and for how long
  • Budget vs. Actual: Compare plan to reality and course-correct early
