Stop Reacting. Start Planning.

Most people only think about taxes in April — but by then, it's too late to change the outcome. Tax planning is a year-round strategy that puts you in control. We work with you proactively to identify opportunities, structure decisions to minimize taxes, and build a long-term plan that grows with your income.

What Tax Planning Includes

  • Entity Selection: Choosing the right business structure (LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp) to minimize self-employment and income taxes
  • Income Shifting: Legally moving income between years or family members to reduce the overall tax burden
  • Retirement Contribution Planning: Maximizing 401(k), SEP-IRA, and HSA contributions to reduce taxable income
  • Deduction Optimization: Identifying and timing deductions for maximum impact
  • Estimated Tax Planning: Calculating and scheduling quarterly payments to avoid underpayment penalties
  • Quarterly Check-Ins: Monitoring your financial picture throughout the year and adjusting strategy as needed
  • Capital Gains Planning: Timing asset sales to minimize short-term vs. long-term tax rates
  • Real Estate & Depreciation: Leveraging depreciation, 1031 exchanges, and rental deductions

Who Benefits Most from Tax Planning?

  • Business owners and self-employed individuals earning $50,000+ net
  • High-income W-2 earners with multiple income streams
  • Real estate investors
  • Individuals approaching major life events (sale of a business, retirement, inheritance)
  • Investors with significant capital gains or losses
💡 The difference planning makes: A business owner earning $150,000 who works with a tax planner year-round can save $10,000–$25,000 annually compared to simply filing at year-end.
