Stress-Free Tax Filing, Done Right

Tax filing doesn't have to be stressful. At Prolific Tax & Multi-Service LLC, we handle every detail of your return — from gathering documents to e-filing and sending you your confirmation — so you can focus on what matters most.

Whether you're a W-2 employee, self-employed, a business owner, or someone who hasn't filed in years, we have the expertise to file accurately and maximize your outcome.

Who We File For

  • Individuals & Families — W-2 earners, retirees, students, and dependents
  • Self-Employed & Freelancers — Schedule C filers, gig workers, 1099 contractors
  • Small Business Owners — LLCs, S-Corps, C-Corps, partnerships, and sole proprietors
  • Nonprofits — Form 990 and tax-exempt filings
  • Multi-State Filers — Nonresident and part-year state returns
  • Back-Year Filers — Haven't filed in years? We specialize in catch-up returns

Our Filing Process

  1. Consultation: We review your situation and identify all income sources and deductions
  2. Document Collection: Secure upload via encrypted portal or in-person drop-off
  3. Preparation: Experienced preparers complete your return using IRS-accepted software
  4. Multi-Step Review: Every return is reviewed for accuracy before filing
  5. Client Review: You review your return and approve before we e-file
  6. E-File & Confirmation: We file and send you your copy with e-file confirmation

What Documents Do You Need?

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Social Security numbers or ITINs for all household members
  • W-2s, 1099s, and any other income statements
  • Last year's tax return (if available)
  • Receipts for deductible expenses (business, medical, charitable)
  • Bank account info for direct deposit of any refund
  • Any IRS or state notices received during the year
💡 Filing remotely? We accept all documents via our secure encrypted portal and complete the entire process by phone or video — no office visit required.

Prior-Year & Amended Returns

If you missed a filing or made an error on a previous return, we can help. We'll review your prior-year returns for errors, missed deductions, or missed credits — and file an amended return (Form 1040-X) if it benefits you. Statutes of limitations apply, so don't wait.
